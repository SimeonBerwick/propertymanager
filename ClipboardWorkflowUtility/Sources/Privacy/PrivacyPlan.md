# Privacy Plan (M5)

## Must-have controls
- Pause capture
- Clear all history
- Retention limit
- Local-only messaging

## Nice next steps
- Ignore source apps by bundle ID
- Detect and skip likely secrets/tokens
- Time-based expiry rules
- Optional secure storage partition for snippets

## Product truth
A clipboard app lives or dies on trust. Privacy controls are not polish; they are core product infrastructure.
