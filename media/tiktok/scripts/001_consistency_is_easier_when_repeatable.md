# Reel 001 — Consistency Is Easier When It’s Repeatable

## Topic
Why most people fail at consistency

## Hook on screen
Most people don’t fail because they lack motivation. They fail because they make everything too hard to repeat.

## Voiceover / captions
Most people think consistency is about staying motivated.
It’s not.

The real problem is that they depend on feeling ready every time.

And if every good habit starts with a bunch of decisions, effort, or setup…
you’re going to fall off.

If you want to work out, make it easy to start.
If you want to make content, keep a list of ideas and use the same format every time.
If you want to make progress faster, make the next step obvious.

Consistency gets easier when the right action is simple to repeat.

So if you keep falling off, stop asking,
“How do I stay motivated?”

Ask,
“How do I make this easier to do again tomorrow?”
