# Kling Prompt — Reel 001

Create a 45–60 second vertical 9:16 TikTok-style video with a clean, modern, faceless productivity aesthetic.

The video should feel:
- practical
- human
- clear
- slightly moody but clean
- fast-paced, but not chaotic

Use 7 short scenes with subtle motion and strong visual clarity.
Do not make it overly cinematic or abstract.
Avoid generic luxury imagery, flashy influencer tropes, or random motivational nonsense.

Theme:
Most people don’t fail because they lack motivation. They fail because they make everything too hard to repeat.

Scene guidance:
1. A tired start to the day — phone alarm, hesitation, low energy
2. A cluttered desk or scattered tasks showing mental friction
3. Too many choices or setup steps creating overwhelm
4. A simpler setup: shoes ready, notebook open, task already prepared
5. A content backlog/template visual showing reduced friction
6. Focused work on one clear next step
7. Final clean end frame with the message: Make the right action easier to repeat

Requirements:
- optimized for overlay captions
- readable composition
- consistent visual palette: black, white, muted blue, soft amber accents
- each scene should support the message of reducing friction
- no baked-in subtitles unless needed minimally
- clean transition into final CTA frame
