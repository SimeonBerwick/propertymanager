## Launch posts

### Introduction

Property maintenance gets messy when requests, texts, vendor replies, photos, approvals, and costs live in different places. Simeonware brings the complete maintenance workflow into one focused workspace for property managers, tenants, maintenance staff, and vendors.

### Product demonstration

One maintenance request in Simeonware can move from tenant intake to manager review, vendor coordination, appointment choices, cost approval, photo evidence, and closeout without losing the history. We will be sharing short, real workflow demonstrations here.

### Founding-manager invitation

We are inviting a limited number of U.S. property managers to try Simeonware for 30 days with a personal onboarding conversation and help importing supported unit and vendor records. No credit card is required and the trial does not automatically become a paid subscription. Use campaign code `AUGUSTFOUNDERS`.

### Workflow example

A tenant reports a leaking kitchen sink with a photo. The manager sees the next action, assigns the right vendor, reviews the vendor response, offers clear appointment choices, keeps approvals and costs with the work order, and closes the job with a complete record. That is the kind of ordinary maintenance work Simeonware is designed to make calmer and easier to audit.
