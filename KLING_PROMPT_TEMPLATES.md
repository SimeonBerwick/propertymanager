# Kling Prompt Templates

## Global style rules
Use these defaults unless a specific video needs otherwise:
- vertical 9:16
- short-form social video
- clear readable composition
- fast pacing
- modern clean aesthetic
- visuals should support the script, not overpower it
- avoid random style switching across weeks
